# Brand Identity & Naming Guidelines

## Product Name

## The Name

### Rationale

* **Authority:** "OS" (Operating System) implies infrastructure, necessity, and scalability. It's not just a tool you use occasionally; it's the foundation you run on.
* **Specificity:** "AI Compliance" clearly defines the problem space without limiting it to just one regulation (like "EU AI Act").
* **Modernity:** Moves away from "Course/Navigator" (passive/educational) to "System/Engine" (active/functional).

## Language Strategy: "AI" vs. "KI"

### The Rule

* **Brand Name:** Always **"AI Compliance OS"**. Never "KI Compliance OS".
  * *Why:* "Compliance OS" is English. Mixing it with German "KI" creates "Denglisch". "AI" also signals modern technology standards (ISO 42001 refers to *AI Systems*).
* **Copy Text (DACH Market):** Use **"KI"** for descriptive text to bridge the gap to the German user.
  * *Examples:* "Ihr Betriebssystem für **KI-Compliance**", "**KI-Management** nach ISO 42001", "**KI-Richtlinien** erstellen".
